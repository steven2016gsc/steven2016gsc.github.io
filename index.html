About Me

Welcome! I'm Sicong (Steven; pron. Sih-tsong) Guo, a 2nd-year master student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree and my first Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My research interests span the areas of control synthesis and verification, cyber-physical systems, optimization, and machine learning.

Outside of my research, I enjoy swimming, watching movies and playing classical piano works by Beethoven, Bach, Mozart, and other masterpieces from the Romantic period.
